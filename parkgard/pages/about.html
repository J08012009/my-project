Our Story

The Insurance Company
Built by Park Workers, for Park Workers

ParkGard was founded because the entertainment industry deserves better than one-size-fits-all coverage.

Why ParkGard Exists

It started with a roller coaster and a broken arm.

In 2019, our founder Jamie Torres was working as a ride operator at a major Central Florida theme park when a coworker suffered a serious on-the-job injury. The coworker — a seasonal employee with no health insurance — faced $40,000 in medical bills with no safety net.

"I watched someone I worked with every day lose everything because our industry treats health insurance like a luxury for full-timers only," Jamie said. "That moment changed everything for me."

After years in the insurance industry, Jamie teamed up with former theme park executives, carnival operators, and content creators to build ParkGard — the first health insurance company purpose-built for the entertainment industry.

Today, ParkGard protects over 50,000 entertainment workers across the country, from Disney World to traveling carnival circuits to YouTube channels with millions of subscribers.
