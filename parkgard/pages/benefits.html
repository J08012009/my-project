Industry-Specific

Benefits Made for Entertainment Workers

These are the benefits that make ParkGard different from every other insurer.

📅

Flexible Season Dates

Start your coverage at the beginning of your season and pause at the end without penalty. Re-enroll the following year in minutes. No other insurer offers this for seasonal workers.

🚗

Multi-State Coverage

Traveling carnival and fair workers move between states constantly. ParkGard follows you — there's no such thing as being "out of network" when you're working in any US state.

🦺

On-the-Job Injury Supplement

Available on Gold and Platinum plans. Supplements your employer's workers' comp for faster claim resolution and additional coverage for medical bills related to workplace accidents.

📹

Creator Equipment Rider

Optional add-on for content creators. Covers medical expenses from equipment-related injuries (camera falls, equipment malfunctions) and provides liability coverage for on-location accidents.

🧘

Mental Health & Wellness

Included on Gold and Platinum plans. Covers therapy sessions, psychiatry, substance use treatment, and stress management programs — because entertainment work can be mentally demanding.

💼

Self-Employed Friendly

Content creators and 1099 workers can deduct ParkGard premiums as a business expense in most cases. We provide documentation to support your tax filing.
